Grado is a tourist town of the North Adriatic Sea, located between Trieste and Venice. It is the favorite destination for many Italian and foreign tourists who are looking for a holiday dedicated to the sun and the sea, family-friendly, faraway from everyday stress.

120 thousand square meters of shores divided into 4 south-facing sunny beaches.

Mitteleuropa's beach since 1892

Already a renowned seaside resort at the end of the 19th century, the Island of Grado is one of the oldest tourist locations in the Upper Adriatic: 10 km of coast with very fine sand entirely facing south, divided into four beaches (Grado Pineta, Spiaggia al Bosco, GIT and Costa Azzurra) which offer differentiated services: VIP areas with large gazebos, baby and family beach with entertainment and play areas, beaches for dogs, bars and restaurants for refreshments, water and beach sports activities, cultural events and musical evenings, for a family-friendly holiday, in a dimension of relaxation and safety, far from the stress of everyday life.
